About Me

Caleb J. Ballard is a Systems Test Engineer at the Johnson Space Center under KBR Inc, collaborating with NASA and international partners. He specializes in capturing system requirements and developing test plans for Mission Control Center Systems (MCCS), including Gateway, ISS, Starliner, and Orion. Caleb has experience in creating detailed test procedures, executing risk evaluations, and maintaining test tools and scripts using DOORS. Previously, he worked as a NASA Flight Controller, gaining expertise in various communication systems for the ISS, developing front-end displays for Mission Control, and updating memory maps for Multiplexer Demultiplexers (MDM). Caleb is currently pursuing a Master of Science in Software Engineering from the University of Houston - Clear Lake (expected May 2026) and possesses a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from the University of Houston (GPA 3.5, May 2022). His technical skills include Python, C++, C, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, GitHub, DOORS, MATLAB, AutoCAD, SharePoint, and Linux. He also has an ISS Command Certification from NASA and was awarded an MSOC excellence award for work contributions.
